## LeadGenie AI

**Effective Date:** April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how LeadGenie AI ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Emmanuel Nnadi, collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our Service.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

**1.1 Information you provide directly:**
- Account information: name, email address, password
- Profile information: your service description (what you offer)
- Lead data: names, email addresses, company names, websites, and notes you enter
- Emails generated and saved within the Service

**1.2 Information collected automatically:**
- Usage data: pages visited, features used, time spent
- Device information: browser type, operating system, IP address
- Cookies and session data for authentication

**1.3 Information from third parties:**
- Authentication data from Supabase (our auth provider)

---

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and operate the Service
- Generate personalized AI emails using your lead context (processed via Anthropic Claude API)
- Send you service-related emails (account confirmation, important updates)
- Improve and develop the Service
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Respond to your support requests
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Ever.

---

### 3. How We Share Your Information

We share your data only in these limited circumstances:

**3.1 Service Providers:**
- **Supabase** — database and authentication (your data is stored in their PostgreSQL database)
- **Anthropic** — AI email generation (lead context is sent to their API to generate emails)
- **Vercel** — hosting and infrastructure

**3.2 Legal Requirements:**
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority.

**3.3 Business Transfer:**
If LeadGenie AI is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before this happens.

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

---

### 4. Data Sent to AI Services

When you generate an email, the following data is sent to the Anthropic Claude API:
- Your service description
- The lead's name, company, website, and notes

This data is used solely to generate the email. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their own privacy policy. We recommend not entering sensitive personal information in lead notes.

---

### 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- All data encrypted in transit via HTTPS
- Database-level Row Level Security (RLS) — you can only access your own data
- Passwords hashed by Supabase Auth (bcrypt)
- API keys stored server-side only, never exposed to the browser
- JWT tokens with short expiry windows

While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

---

### 6. Data Retention

- **Account data** — retained as long as your account is active
- **Lead and email data** — retained as long as your account is active
- **Deleted data** — permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Usage logs** — retained for 90 days for debugging purposes
